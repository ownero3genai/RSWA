OUR STORY

About Us

Real Estate is the center of India's growing economy today. There is abundance of opportunities and Realtors play a very important role in making sure that the deals go through without much hassle.

Time has come for India to be globally leading in terms of Real Estate and an Organized way of working can help us achieve that.

Real Estate in general goes through a cycle of processes till the time a deal is closed. Realtors have to take care of clients with wide range of expectations and fulfill their requirement. Being professional and following a process oriented work flow can help us close the deals.

REALTORS SOCIAL WELFARE ASSOCIATION (RSWA) was formed to provide realtors a platform to perform their operation in a process oriented manner and grow their business.

Registered Association Formally registered under Maharashtra Societies Registration Act, Reg. No. Maharashtra/2000/16/Thane.
RERA Compliance Cell Dedicated RERA cell established to protect members and ensure compliance with real estate regulations.
Democratic Governance Elected leadership renewed periodically through transparent democratic elections.

OUR CORE PURPOSE

Our Vision & Mission

OUR VISION

To be India's most trusted and respected realtor association — a beacon of professional integrity, innovation, and social responsibility. We envision a real estate industry where every practitioner operates with transparency, buyers and sellers transact with confidence, and RSWA members lead the transformation of Maharashtra's property landscape through ethical standards and collaborative excellence.

OUR MISSION

To provide realtors a platform to perform their operations in a process-oriented manner and grow their business. We empower real estate professionals through comprehensive education, robust welfare programs, and meaningful networking — fostering a community where every member can succeed and contribute to a stronger, more credible real estate ecosystem across Maharashtra.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold honesty and transparency in all dealings — with members, developers, regulators, and the public. Trust is the foundation of everything we do.

Solidarity

We believe in the power of collective action. Every member's strength contributes to the whole, and we stand together in protecting the interests of our community.

Excellence

We continuously raise the bar through professional development, training programs, and best practices that keep our members ahead of the curve.

Fairness

We ensure equitable treatment for all members regardless of seniority, geography, or background. Our mediation processes are impartial and principled.

Growth

We are committed to the professional and personal growth of every member through resources, mentorship, and opportunities that expand their capabilities.

Welfare

Beyond professional support, we care deeply about the well-being of our members and their families — providing social welfare programs and community assistance.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones That Shaped Us

2010

RSWA Founded

A group of dedicated real estate professionals from Andheri come together to form the Realtors Social Welfare Association with a founding charter focused on member welfare and ethical practice.

2012

First Branch Opened

Growing demand from the Western corridor leads to the opening of the first branch office in Virar West, bringing new members into the fold and extending the association's reach.

2015

500 Member Milestone

RSWA crosses 500 registered members — a landmark achievement celebrated with a grand annual summit attended by prominent real estate developers and government officials.

2018

RERA Cell Established

Following the Real Estate Regulation Act's implementation, RSWA establishes a dedicated RERA Compliance Cell staffed by legal experts to help members navigate the new regulatory landscape.

2020

Palghar & Boisar Expansion

Recognizing rapid industrial and residential development in the northern corridor, RSWA opens new branches in Palghar and Boisar, extending the association's reach across the entire Western rail corridor.

TODAY

A Thriving Network

RSWA today serves 500+ active members across multiple branches — having empowered thousands of real estate professionals across Maharashtra with education, legal support, and community welfare.
