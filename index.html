Who We Are

A Trusted Network of Real Estate Professionals

Founded in 2010, RSWA — Realtors Social Welfare Association — is the premier organization representing real estate professionals across the Mumbai–Vasai–Virar–Palghar–Boisar corridor.

We champion the rights of channel partners, facilitate fair trade practices, and provide our members with the tools, knowledge, and community they need to thrive in a competitive market.

RERA Support & Compliance Dedicated RERA cell guiding members through regulatory requirements and registrations.
Legal Aid & Dispute Resolution Access to experienced real estate lawyers for contract disputes, documentation, and mediation.
Powerful Professional Network Connect with 350+ verified realtors, builders, and consultants across 4 branches.

Membership Advantages

Our Members Get

Joining RSWA gives you access to a comprehensive ecosystem of support, resources, and professional opportunities.

Strong Networking

Build lasting relationships with verified real estate professionals, builders, and investors across the Western corridor through regular events and meetups.

RERA & Legal Support

Navigate RERA compliance with ease. Get expert guidance on registrations, project approvals, and stay updated on the latest regulatory changes.

Training Programs

Sharpen your skills through workshops on digital marketing, negotiation, property valuation, and legal documentation conducted by industry experts.

Exclusive Property Inventory

Get early and exclusive access to new project launches, developer inventories, and co-brokerage opportunities only available to RSWA members.

Financial Assistance

Access welfare funds, emergency financial support, and facilitated loan programs designed specifically for association members and their families.

Dispute Resolution

Our experienced mediation committee helps resolve commission disputes, contract disagreements, and inter-member conflicts fairly and efficiently.
