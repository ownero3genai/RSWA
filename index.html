Who We Are

The Most Trusted Network in Western Maharashtra Real Estate

Founded in 2010, RSWA — Realtors Social Welfare Association — is the premier organization representing real estate professionals across the Mumbai–Vasai–Virar–Palghar–Boisar corridor.

We champion the rights of channel partners, facilitate fair trade practices, and provide our members with the tools, knowledge, and community they need to thrive in a competitive market.

RERA Support & Compliance Dedicated RERA cell guiding members through regulatory requirements and registrations.
Legal Aid & Dispute Resolution Access to experienced real estate lawyers for contract disputes, documentation, and mediation.
Powerful Professional Network Connect with 350+ verified realtors, builders, and consultants across 4 branches.
