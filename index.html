About RSWA

The Realtors Social Welfare Association (RSWA) is Maharashtra's leading professional body for real estate practitioners. Founded in 2018, we have grown into a powerful network of over 500 active members across the state, united by our commitment to professional excellence and ethical practice.

As an ISO 9001:2015 certified organization, RSWA maintains the highest standards in everything we do — from member services and industry advocacy to education and welfare programs. Our certification reflects our dedication to quality and continuous improvement in serving India's real estate community.

We provide our members with unparalleled networking opportunities, professional development resources, legal support, and a strong collective voice in shaping real estate policy across Maharashtra and beyond.

OUR CORE VALUES

Our Vision & Mission

OUR VISION

To be India's most trusted and respected realtor association — a beacon of professional integrity, innovation, and social responsibility. We envision a real estate industry where every practitioner operates with transparency, where buyers and sellers transact with confidence, and where RSWA members lead the transformation of Maharashtra's property landscape through ethical standards and collaborative excellence.

OUR MISSION

To empower realtors across Maharashtra through comprehensive education, robust welfare programs, and meaningful networking opportunities. We are committed to advocating for fair policies that protect both professionals and consumers, providing access to legal resources and industry insights, and fostering a community where every member can grow, succeed, and contribute to a stronger, more credible real estate ecosystem in India.
