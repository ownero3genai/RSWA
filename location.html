About Mumbai HQ

The Founding Branch

The Mumbai Headquarters is the founding office of RSWA, established in January 2010 by 18 pioneering real estate professionals who sought to create a formal welfare association for channel partners in the Western suburbs.

Located in Andheri West — the heart of Mumbai's real estate activity — the headquarters serves as the administrative center for all RSWA operations, housing the president, general secretary, and the core executive committee.

The HQ office offers walk-in services for RERA compliance assistance, legal consultation, documentation support, and member networking. It also hosts the majority of RSWA's major events including the Annual Summit and General Body Meetings.
