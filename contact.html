Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often from new and prospective members.

To become an RSWA member, you can fill out the contact form on this page or visit any of our 4 branch offices. You'll need to provide proof of your real estate credentials (RERA registration, channel partner agreement, etc.), a recent photograph, and pay the annual membership fee. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 5 working days.
RSWA membership fees are structured based on member category: Individual Channel Partner: ₹2,500/year, Property Consultant: ₹2,500/year, Builder/Developer: ₹5,000/year, Corporate Member: ₹10,000/year. Life membership is also available. Contact us for current rates and any available concessions for first-time members.
Yes, absolutely. RSWA operates a dedicated RERA Compliance Cell at the Mumbai HQ office. Members can walk in during RERA consultation hours (Monday – Friday, 10 AM – 1 PM) to get guidance on RERA agent registration, project registration, renewal procedures, and compliance documentation. We also host periodic workshops on RERA updates.
RSWA has an experienced Dispute Resolution Committee comprising senior members, legal experts, and industry veterans. When a member files a dispute (commission non-payment, contract breach, inter-member conflicts), the committee initiates a mediation process. Most disputes are resolved within 2–4 weeks through structured mediation. For unresolved cases, RSWA can facilitate legal proceedings with our empanelled attorneys.
Some RSWA events like the Annual Property Expo and major conferences are open to real estate professionals and the general public. However, member-exclusive events such as the Annual General Body Meeting, RERA workshops, and networking meets are for verified RSWA members only. Check individual event listings for eligibility details. Non-members interested in attending member-only events can apply for a temporary guest pass — contact us for details.
