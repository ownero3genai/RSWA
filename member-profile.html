About Rajesh Sharma

Rajesh Sharma is one of the founding members and the current President of RSWA. With over 22 years of experience in the Mumbai real estate market, Rajesh has built an exceptional reputation as a trusted channel partner specializing in residential and commercial properties across the Western suburbs.

His journey in real estate began in 2002 when he joined his family's property business in Andheri. Recognizing the need for a formal professional network, Rajesh was instrumental in founding RSWA in 2010 along with 17 other industry professionals.

Under his presidency, RSWA has grown from 18 founding members to over 350 active professionals, established 4 branch offices, and facilitated more than ₹500 Crores in property transactions.
